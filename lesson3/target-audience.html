Target Audience

Our main target audience would be locals, but we also want to specifically target men and women who are invested in the community and enjoying going out and exploring new things to do in the area. Our main demographic will be around 20 years old to 60 years old that make around 50k or more anually. We also want to target people who have a social media presence.
